Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I schedule a consultation with your team?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, filling out the contact form on this page, or emailing us directly. We typically respond within 24 hours to set up an appointment at your convenience.

What are your business hours?

Our standard business hours are Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Which areas do you serve for real estate services?

We primarily serve the Mumbai metropolitan area, including all major suburbs. For select projects and clients, we also operate in Pune, Hyderabad, and Bangalore. Contact us to discuss your specific location needs.

How long does it typically take to get loan approval?

Loan approval times vary based on the type of loan and completeness of documentation. Personal loans can be approved in 24-48 hours, while home loans typically take 5-7 working days after all documents are submitted.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet for all our services. This includes real estate viewings, loan consultations, and legal advice sessions.
